Gap Analysis — what it measures & how to read it
Compares your company's skill adoption rates against the Stack Overflow 2025 Developer Survey benchmark. For each technology, Gap = Industry % − Company %. A positive gap means the industry uses that technology more than your team does; a negative gap means your team leads the industry.
🔴 Gap (>5 pp) → training or hiring priority 🟡 Near (±5 pp) → watch & maintain 🟢 Strength (<−5 pp) → competitive advantage

Trend Alignment — what it measures & how to read it
Maps your team's current skills against what the industry wants to learn next, drawn from SO 2025 "want to work with" responses. The scatter plot places each technology into one of four strategic quadrants based on industry desire and your company's current coverage.
🚀 Invest — high demand, low coverage → hire or train urgently 💪 Leverage — strong coverage, high demand → highlight in JDs 👀 Monitor — low demand, low coverage → keep an eye ✅ Maintain — strong coverage, lower demand → stable, no action

Category Coverage — what it measures & how to read it
Shows how broadly your team spans major technology domains (Languages, Databases, Cloud & DevOps, Frameworks, etc.). Coverage % = employees with at least one skill in that category ÷ total headcount × 100.
Narrow radar → over-specialised team, exposure risk Low Cloud/DevOps in an eng team → common risk signal Aim for balanced coverage across your key domains
